About Me

Hello There! Mahabubar Rahman is an experienced Data Scientist and Research Associate with a strong academic background in Statistics and Computer Science. I hold a Bachelor's and Master's degree in Statistics, and I am currently pursuing my Master's degree in Computer Science with a major in Data Science. With a passion for leveraging data to drive actionable insights, I have been providing data analytics solutions since 2020. I thrive on solving complex problems and uncovering patterns and trends hidden within large datasets. My expertise lies in utilizing advanced statistical techniques, machine learning algorithms, and data visualization tools to extract meaningful information and make data-driven decisions. Throughout my academic journey and professional experience, I have honed my skills in data preprocessing, exploratory data analysis, predictive modeling, and optimization. I am proficient in programming languages such as Python and R, and I have hands-on experience with popular data science libraries and frameworks. My dedication to staying updated with the latest advancements in the field of data science allows me to continuously expand my knowledge and adapt to new challenges. I am an avid learner, always seeking opportunities to enhance my skill set and explore emerging technologies. Collaboration and effective communication are at the core of my work approach. I thrive in interdisciplinary environments, where I can collaborate with diverse teams to extract insights from data and translate them into practical solutions that drive business growth and improve decision-making processes. If you are looking for a passionate and results-driven Data Scientist with a strong academic foundation and industry experience, I would be delighted to connect with you. Let's explore how we can leverage the power of data together to unlock valuable insights and achieve success. Feel free to reach out to me to discuss potential collaborations, projects, or opportunities in the exciting field of data science. Research Interests: • Statistical modeling and analysis • Machine learning and data mining • Artificial intelligence and deep learning • Big data processing and analysis • Natural language processing and text mining • Statistical programming and data visualization
